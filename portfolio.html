Munchkin Power Level

I finally started my javascript journey and this is the first real thing I've made with it following the scrimba JS module. While their project was different I didn't really want to do the same thing so I altered it to something I care about and something I could use. Munchkin is a card game you play and the hardest part of the game is keeping track of your power in the game. Power is how you fight different monsters and there are hundreds of different cards that dictate your power as well the monster's power. This simple counter uses javascript functions and lets those functions write to the webpage. Keeps track of current and past power as you save.
